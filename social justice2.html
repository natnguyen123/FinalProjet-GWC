Again, this affected schools, causing discrimination and segregation.

Discrimination In Schools

According to a study, teachers were shown videos of preschool students.
There was a white boy and girl and a black boy and girl.
The teachers were instructed to find any bad behavior in the students.
There was no bad behavior in any of the students, but in
the results of the study, it was shown that most teachers claimed to
have seen more bad behavior in the black children rather than
the white children. Students also discriminate against their black classmates.
